Latitude vs. Max Temperature

When comparing a sample of 546 randomly selected cities, there appears to be a clear relationship between latitude and max Temperature in Fahrenheit. Cities above 20 degrees latitude tend to see lower max temperatures. These temperatures seem to gradually increase as the latitude increases. Meanwhile, cities below the equator experience consistently higher max temperatures.

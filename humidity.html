Latitude vs. Humidity

When comparing a sample of 546 randomly selected cities, there appears to be higher humidity percentages closer to the equator. Once the latitude of a city is above 20 degrees or below 20 degrees, the distribution of humitiy percentages increases. In other words, it appears that the equator tends to experience higher humidity more consistently than other latitudes.
